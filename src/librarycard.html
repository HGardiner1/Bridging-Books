Want to obtain a Charlottesville library card?

  1. 1. Visit a JMRL Branch: You can obtain a library card by visiting any of the JMRL branches in person.
  2. 2. Photo ID: Bring a photo ID that includes your name and current address.
  3. 3. Proof of Residence: If your photo ID doesn't have your current address, you may need to provide additional proof of residence, such as a utility bill or lease agreement.
  4. 4. Registration: Complete the library card application form and provide any required information.

Additional Information

  • Out-of-Area Fee: There is an annual out-of-area fee of $30 if you're not in one of the following cities:
    • Charlottesville
    • Albemarle
    • Louisa
    • Greene
    • Nelson
  • Temporary Card: You'll receive access to ebooks, audiobooks, and databases.
  • In-House Use Card: Allows you to use the library's facilities but not borrow materials.
